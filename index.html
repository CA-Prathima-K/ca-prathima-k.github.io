About Me

I am a Chartered Accountant with over 10 years of post-qualification experience in assurance, taxation, transfer pricing, FEMA advisory, customs regulations, GST, litigation support, due diligence, and regulatory consulting. I am a Partner at RSKP and Co. and lead the Chennai practice of the firm, serving clients across manufacturing, services, trading, export-oriented businesses, multinational groups, SEZ units, and high-growth enterprises. Over the years, I have led complex assignments involving statutory audits, Ind AS implementation, transfer pricing, GST advisory, customs and DGFT matters, FEMA compliance, due diligence engagements, and tax litigation.
