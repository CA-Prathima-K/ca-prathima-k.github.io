About Me

I am a Chartered Accountant with over 10 years of post-qualification experience in assurance, taxation, regulatory advisory, transfer pricing, indirect taxes, customs, FEMA, business compliance and strategic advisory services.

I lead the Chennai practice of RSKP and Co., serving manufacturing, services, technology, trading, export-oriented businesses, SEZ units and multinational groups. My experience spans statutory audits, Ind AS reporting, transfer pricing, direct and indirect taxes, due diligence, litigation support, customs and DGFT regulations, FEMA advisory and corporate compliance.
